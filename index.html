GroovyServby kobo project

GroovyServ makes startup time quicker, by pre-invoking groovy as a TCP/IP server. (If you know gnuserv/gnuclient, this is like that.)

In the case of scripting in dynamic-type languages, quick response about invocation is very important. Try-and-run cycles is repeated frequently than static-type languages, so sometimes 2 seconds or even 1 second might be intolerable.

GroovyServ reduces the startup time of JVM and Groovy runtime significantly. It's at least 10 to 20 times faster than regular Groovy.
