GroovyServ by kobo project

GroovyServ (aka GroovyServer) make startup time quicker, by pre-invoking groovy as a TCP/IP server.(If you know gnuserv/gnuclient, this is like that.)

For writing script, for example by using groovy, quick response is very important. Because it has less static type checking, try-and-run loop is repeated frequently. Sometimes 2 seconds or even 1 second might be intolerable.

GroovyServer reduces the stetup time of JVM and groovy runtime, and makes the start time 10 to 20 times faster.
